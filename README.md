# About
A Minecraft datapack that contains many unique kits I use in some of my other datapacks, including:
1. Starter Kit (Stone Tools, Leather Armour, 64 Steak)
2. Barebones PvP (Iron Sword, Iron Axe, Iron Armour, 8-16 Golden Apples, 64 Steak)
3. Bow PvP (Bow with Infinity, 1 Arrow, 8-16 Golden Apples, 64 Steak, Leather Armour)
4. Classic PvP (Iron Sword, Fishing Rod, Bow, Iron Axe, 8–16 Golden Apples, 32 Arrows, 64 Cooked Beef, 64 Cobblestone, Shield, Iron Armour)
5. Instant Crossbow PvP (Crossbow with Quick Charge 6 and Multishot 1, 512 Arrows, 8–16 Golden Apples, 64 Cooked Beef, Protection 4 Diamond Armour)
6. Instant Firework Crossbow PvP (same as Instant Crossbow PvP kit but with 512 Firework Rockets with multiple Large Ball explosions instead of 512 Arrows)
7. UHC PvP (Diamond Sword with Sharpness 3–5, Fishing Rod, Bow with Power 3–5, Diamond Pickaxe with Efficiency 3–5, 8–16 Golden Apples, 64 Cooked Beef, Lava Bucket, 64 Cobblestone, Water Bucket, Protection 2-4 Diamond Armour, Anvil, Enchanting Table, 15 Bookshelves, 64 Oak Logs, Diamond Axe with Sharpness 3–5 and Efficiency 3–5, 32 Arrows, Shield)
